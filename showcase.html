Code Institute Milestone Project-1
Sponge/Madeira
  • Self-raising Flour
  • Butter
  • Caster Suger
  • Eggs
  • Baking Powder
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C / gas mark 4.
  2. Measure all the ingredients into a large bowl.
  3. Mix all of the ingredients using a electric whisk.
  4. Pour the mixture into 2 non-stick 7 inch (18cm) tins.
  5. Place them in the oven till golden brown 15-25 minutes.
  6. Cool on a wire rack before serving.
Boiled Chocolate Cake
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Bicarbonate of Soda
  • Cocoa
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Self-raising Flour
  1. Place first five ingredients into a large saucepan.
  2. Bring to the boil and simmer for 3 minutes.
  3. Allow to cool slightly.
  4. Add eggs and flour, and stir well.
  5. Pour into a greased 20 cm cake pan.
  6. Bake at 180C for 50 minutes.
  7. Ice when cool if desired.
Chocolate Biscuit Cake
  • Butter
  • Chocolate
  • Digestive Biscuits
  • Rich Tea Biscuits
  • Golden Syrup
  • Optional: Fruit/Nuts/Malteasers
  1. Line a 15cms/6" round cake tin or a 2lb loaf tin with a double layer of greaseproof paper.
  2. Melt the butter, syrup and chocolate in a pan over a low heat. Stir to make sure all the ingredients are well mixed together.
  3. Add the biscuits, maltesers and fruit and nuts, if used. Stir well.
  4. Transfer to prepared tin. Level it on top and press down well to avoid air gaps. Allow to get cold and hard. Wrap completely in greaseproof paper and store in a fridge.
